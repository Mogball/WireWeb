Pay faster and earn more

Experience a simpler and more rewarding way
to pay and manage your finances.

Enjoy a truly borderless world with Vire.

Learn what Vire has to offer

Fee-less

With zero transaction fees and no monthly charges, Vire is the best way to pay for goods and services or send money to friends and family, online or in person.

Extra Credit points

Get the most out of your money with Vire Extra Credit. Earn 1% extra value on each deposit you make in your Vire Wallet, and spend Extra Credit as you would money.

Versatile

Use Vire anywhere in the world, with any currency you chose, and with anybody you want. Pay with the mobile app or online, at Vire terminals or ATMs.

How to use Vire

1

Download the app from Google Play, and sign up using only your email or phone number.

2

Add funds to your Vire Wallet by withdrawing from your bank account or by visiting a Vire ATM.

3

Start sending money immediately to peers or paying for goods and services, in person or online.

Multi-platform

phonelink_ring

Mobile

Pay faster with Vire Mobile. Use tap-to-pay or QR scanning to make quick and secure transactions at stores or with other users. Manage your finances and utilities from the Mobile Dashboard with a few easy clicks.

web

Web

Process and manage transactions on a larger scale on Vire Online. Send invoices, pay bills, and track your financial progress from the online Dashboard, which also provides in-depth analysis of your finances. Tweak user settings and personalize your Vire Account.

business

For business

Vire not only gives store owners a faster way to accept payments, but also provides a platform to connect with Vire users. Use Vire-specific transaction tools to make payments faster and easier for you and your customers, and track your finances using the Dashboard.

work

API and Platform

Vire provides a well-documented API that you may embed in your programs to make payments easier for your clients and their customers. Vire also has an open-source platform on which you may develop your own applications.
