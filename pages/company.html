Payment Methods

Vire gives you the option to save payment methods, such as credit or debit cards, to your Wallet for convenience. You can add or remove payment methods from the Dashboard.

Send or request money

You can send and receive money through your Vire account using Vire online. You can send, request, accept, or deny funds from your Personal Board.

Mobile App

You can find Vire Mobile on Google Play

Tap-to-Pay

In order to use Vire Mobile's tap function, your phone must have NFC (near field communication). Click here to see if your phone supports NFC. Otherwise, you can always use QR codes.

Account

To use Vire Mobile, you need only a phone number or email to sign up. You can sign up online here.

Borrowing and lending

Vire lets you borrow and lend funds. Vire uses advanced algorithms to determine the best rates for you and other users. Loyal and consistent users wil be rewarded with better rates. Check your eligibility at Vire Services.

Insurance and mortgage

Vire provides insurance and mortgage services. Like with borrowing and lending, Vire uses a series of algorithms. Check your eligibility at Vire Services.
